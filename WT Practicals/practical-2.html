Different Types of Marquees

1. Scrolling Marquee (Default):

Welcome to the world of marquees! This is a default scrolling marquee.

2. Marquee with Direction (Left):

This marquee moves from right to left.

3. Marquee with Direction (Right):

This marquee moves from left to right.

4. Marquee with Direction (Up):

This marquee moves from bottom to top.

5. Marquee with Direction (Down):

This marquee moves from top to bottom.

6. Marquee with Scrolling Speed:

This marquee has a faster scroll speed.

7. Marquee with Looping:

This marquee will repeat 5 times.

8. Marquee with Background Color:

This marquee has a background color.

9. Marquee with Width:

This marquee is limited to 50% of the screen width.

10. Marquee with Behavior (Alternate):

This marquee bounces back and forth.

11. Marquee with Behavior (Slide):

This marquee slides in and stops.
